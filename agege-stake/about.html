ABOUT US

"Called to Serve"

Missionaries of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints are dedicated individuals who are "Called to Serve" and spread the teachings of Jesus Christ and the principles of the Church.


Typically, Young men and Young women volunteer to serve a mission lasting 18 Months for Young Women and 24 months for Young Men, during which they engage in community service, teach gospel principles, and share their faith with others.


They often learn new languages and immerse themselves in different cultures, aiming to foster understanding and build relationships while promoting spiritual growth.

OUR OBJECTIVES

We are set to achieve the following laudable objectives:

Our Primary objectives as Missionaries of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is to "Invite other to come unto Christ and recieve the restored gospel through Faith in the lord Jesus Christ and his Atonement, Repentance, Baptism, Recieving the gift of the Holy Ghost and Enduring to the end."

We aim to provide service to communities, strengthen families, and encourage moral values. Additionally, missionaries strive to support local congregations, assist in humanitarian efforts, and promote education and self-reliance among those they serve.

Our ultimate goal is to inspire faith and positive change in the lives of individuals and communities.

LEADERS QUOTES

"Missionary Work is Divine"

1. Elder Jeffrey R. Holland (Acting President of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles): "The greatest generation of missionaries is yet to come. They will be the ones who realize that they are called to serve and that the Lord will help them."
2. Elder David A. Bednar (Member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles): "Missionary work is not just about the numbers; it is about the individuals we serve and the relationships we build."
3. President Gordon B. Hinckley (Late President & Prophet, Seer & Revelator of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints): "Every member a missionary. That is our goal. We want every member of the Church to be involved in this great work."
4. President Russell M. Nelson (President, Prophet, Seer & Revelator of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints): "As we serve others, we come to know the Savior better. We learn that we are called to serve."
5. Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf (Member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles): "When we are in the service of our fellow beings, we are in the service of God. This is the essence of missionary work."
